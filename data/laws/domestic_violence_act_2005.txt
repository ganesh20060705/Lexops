Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

--------------------------------------------------

Section 2 - Definitions

Defines domestic relationship, aggrieved person, and respondent.

Explanation:
Clarifies who is protected under the Act.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 3 - Domestic violence

Includes:
- Physical abuse
- Emotional abuse
- Economic abuse

Explanation:
Broad definition ensures comprehensive protection.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 12 - Application to Magistrate

Victim can file application seeking protection orders.

Explanation:
Starts legal proceedings for protection.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 17 - Right to residence

Woman has the right to reside in shared household.

Explanation:
Prevents forced eviction.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 18 - Protection orders

Court can restrain abuser from committing violence.

Explanation:
Provides immediate legal protection.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 19 - Residence orders

Court can ensure safe residence for victim.

Explanation:
Secures housing rights.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 20 - Monetary relief

Court may order financial support for victim.

Explanation:
Covers medical expenses, loss of earnings, etc.

--------------------------------------------------
