Indian Penal Code (IPC), 1860

--------------------------------------------------

Section 302 - Punishment for murder

Whoever commits murder shall be punished with death or life imprisonment.

Explanation:
Applies in cases involving intentional killing.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 354 - Assault on woman

Assault or criminal force against a woman with intent to outrage modesty.

Explanation:
Used in cases of harassment or inappropriate physical conduct.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 376 - Rape

Defines and punishes the offence of rape.

Explanation:
Covers serious sexual offences with strict punishment.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 420 - Cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property

Punishes cheating involving fraud and deception.

Explanation:
Commonly used in financial fraud and scam cases.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 498A - Cruelty by husband or relatives

Punishes cruelty or harassment of a married woman.

Explanation:
Used in domestic abuse and dowry-related cases.

--------------------------------------------------
