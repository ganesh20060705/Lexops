Consumer Protection Act, 2019

--------------------------------------------------

Section 2 - Definitions

Defines key terms such as:
- Consumer
- Service
- Deficiency
- Complaint

Explanation:
This section clarifies who can file complaints and what constitutes consumer harm.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 18 - Consumer Protection Councils

Establishes councils to promote and protect consumer rights.

Explanation:
These councils guide policy and awareness for consumer protection.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 34 - Jurisdiction of District Commission

District Commission handles cases where value is within prescribed limits.

Explanation:
This helps consumers know where to file complaints.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 35 - Filing of complaint

Consumers can file complaints regarding:
- Defective goods
- Deficient services
- Unfair trade practices

Explanation:
This section provides the formal process to initiate legal action.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 38 - Procedure on admission

Commission examines complaint and may:
- Admit or reject it
- Order investigation

Explanation:
Ensures structured processing of consumer complaints.

--------------------------------------------------
