Information Technology Act, 2000

--------------------------------------------------

Section 43 - Damage to computer systems

Covers unauthorized access, data theft, or system damage.

Explanation:
Applies to hacking and cyber intrusions.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 66 - Computer-related offences

Enhances penalties for offences under Section 43 when done dishonestly.

Explanation:
Used in cybercrime involving intent to cause harm.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 66C - Identity theft

Punishes misuse of passwords, digital signatures, or identity.

Explanation:
Applies in online fraud and impersonation cases.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 67 - Publishing obscene content

Punishes publishing or transmitting obscene material online.

Explanation:
Used in cases of inappropriate digital content.

--------------------------------------------------

Section 72 - Breach of confidentiality

Punishes unauthorized disclosure of information.

Explanation:
Protects user data and privacy.

--------------------------------------------------
